About Me

My name is Aaron Abramson. I am originally from Los Angeles where I had a 12 year career in IT at various companies and projects. I moved to the Central Valley with my girlfriend in May of 2021 and in April 2022 I decided to go back to school to pursue a new career in programming.

I have always been fascinated with technology at a young age and enjoyed building computers and working with gadgets. I hope that by going back to school I can develop a comprehensive understanding of computer technology and transition into a career that is focused on programming, automation, and data science.

I enjoy videogames, traveling with my girlfriend, photography, and trying new foods.
